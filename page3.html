Decomposition

We worked on Decomposition as well!

Decomposition is when you break your big problem into smaller problems.

For example: My friend invited my for a sleep over.

In order to do this I will have to break it down.

And I will end with a list of things to solve: Look where the house of my friend is,Get the permission from my parents,know at wich time my parents will pick me up,and what do I need to bring to the sleepover.

I will then have to solve all those little problems.

When all of this is solved my big problem is solved,this process is called Decomposition.

In class, Our teacher gave us a problem: build a tower with the resources.

We had: staws,sicors,rulers,tape,gluesticks,paper and string.

In my group we did a tall tower with a paper boat in top of it. We used: 4 rulers,tape,5 straws,4 gluesticks,1 piece of paper and tape.

The class had 5 minutes of planning and 15 minutes of building.
